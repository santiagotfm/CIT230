Facial Procedures

Blepharoplasty:

Cheek Enhancement:
This procedure corrects excess skin, tissue and fat on the eyelids. This will change the look in both the upper and lower eyelids. This procedure removes the fat bags from the cheeks and improves the facial contour.
   

Dermabrasion:

Forehead Lift:
This procedure regularizes the most superficial layers of the skin, giving it a smoother appearance. This procedure is a rejuvenation of the forehead. It reduces wrinkles that occur in the area.
   

Mentoplasty:

Otoplasty:
This procedure improves the projection of the chin, to give facial harmony. This procedure improves the form of the ears, its position and proportion.
   

Rhinoplasty:

 
This procedure resolves aesthetic problems of the nose such as deviations and malformations.
